About FairMed

FairMed International was founded on a simple observation: hospitals in Nigeria pay too much for equipment they desperately need.

The current system is broken. Devices pass through multiple intermediaries — brokers, regional distributors, local agents — each adding their margin. By the time equipment reaches a hospital, it costs 2-3x the original price.

We cut through that. We work directly with certified European refurbishers who test, calibrate, and certify every device. We handle the logistics, documentation, and NAFDAC registration support. You get professional equipment at fair prices.

What "Refurbished" Means

Professionally inspected, repaired, tested, and certified. Every device includes calibration certificates, maintenance records, and original documentation. CE-marked. ISO 13485 compliant suppliers.

Our Guarantee

1-year warranty on all equipment. If something goes wrong, we fix it or replace it. 3-year lamp replacement guarantee on phototherapy units. 24-hour WhatsApp support.

NAFDAC & Import Support

We provide all documentation required for NAFDAC registration and customs clearance. CE certificates, calibration reports, proforma invoices, certificates of origin — everything you need.
