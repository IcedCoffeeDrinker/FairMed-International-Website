# Content Writing

## Request: $ARGUMENTS

## FairMed International Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Professional** — We're selling to hospitals and healthcare facilities
- **Trustworthy** — Emphasize certifications, warranties, track record
- **Direct** — No fluff, get to the point
- **Specific** — Numbers, prices, timeframes over vague claims

### Avoid
- Superlatives: "best", "leading", "revolutionary"
- Hype: "cutting-edge", "state-of-the-art", "world-class"
- Vague claims: "affordable", "high-quality", "excellent service"
- Consumer marketing speak

### Use Instead
- Specific numbers: "$1,500", "1-year warranty", "48-hour response"
- Concrete benefits: "Works during power outages", "NAFDAC registration included"
- Professional terms: "certified", "refurbished", "CE-marked"

## Key Messages

### Why Choose FairMed?
1. **Direct pricing** — No broker/distributor markup (20-40% savings)
2. **Certified equipment** — CE-marked, ISO 13485 compliant suppliers
3. **Full support** — Warranty, NAFDAC assistance, training materials
4. **Nigeria-ready** — Battery backup, local language support, WhatsApp service

### Product Categories
- Neonatal care (phototherapy, warmers, resuscitation)
- Diagnostic (monitors, ECG, ultrasound)
- General medical (infusion pumps, examination equipment)

### Trust Signals
- "Certified refurbished equipment"
- "1-year warranty included"
- "NAFDAC registration support"
- "24-hour WhatsApp support"
